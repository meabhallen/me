Méabh Allen

Welcome to my personal webpage! Here you can learn more about my research interests.

I'm a condensed matter theory PhD candidate in Joel Moore's group at UC Berkeley. I have a B.Sc. in mathematics and physics, and an M.Sc. in quantum field theory. I'm interested in universality in non-equilibrium quantum systems. At the moment I'm using tensor networks and field theory to study novel dynamical behaviour in critical spin systems.
