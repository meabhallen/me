Méabh Allen

I'm a condensed matter theory PhD candidate in Joel Moore's group at UC Berkeley. I study universality in non-equilibrium quantum systems, using field theory and tensor networks to uncover novel dynamical behavior in critical spin systems.

I care deeply about these questions both for their fundamental value and for their applications in quantum computing and metrology. I'm a 2024 recipient of the NSF Challenge Institute for Quantum Computation (CIQC) Seed Funding.

Outside of physics, I'm a lifelong equestrian, avid gardener, graceless yogi, and bookworm.
